Dustless Sanding Wood Floors

25 June 2013

Just Floored, LLC offers professional wood floor refinishing using the "dustless sanding" method and hardwood floor installation for homeowners and business owners to keep your house clean while installing or refinishing your wood floors. Not only does this "green" method of sanding keep the micro particles created when sanding down, it helps keep the fumes down. You will not only love the refinishing floor job and installation job by our highly trained professionals, but you will enjoy the safety of clean air. Just Floored uses hoses connected to suction machines to keep the dust moving outside your home. If you have ever had hardwood floors installed in a residence you live in, or had the wood floors sanded for refinishing purpose, you will appreciate the difference "dustless sanding" makes to your indoor air quality.
