Hurricane Matthew Floor Repair

29 October 2016

Just Floored LLC is ready, willing and able to assist homeowners of the North Florida Atlantic Coast with restoring, refinishing or replacing their hardwood floors that may have been damaged by Hurricane Matthew from water damage, wind, sand or other problems arising from strong wind, rain, hail or flooding.

We can come out to your home or business in areas such as Jacksonville, and bring the showroom to your home. We can work with your general contractor or insurance company. We can also assist you in selecting the right hardwood floors species such as cherry, maple, oak, hand scraped, hickory and more, hardwood plank style and stain color for new hardwood floor installations. We work with solid hardwood floors, engineered wood flooring, hand scraped floors, bamboo, cork, vinyl planks and tile or stone.

Just Floored LLC offers professional hardwood floor refinishing which includes dustless sanding of the hardwoods to remove blemishes, gouges and other marks, as well as remove the current finish and stain. Using the "Dustless Sanding" method helps reduce the amount of dust that would happen from sanding and helps keep your indoor air clean. We also offer Low VOC's or No VOC's in products in the floor refinishing process for a clean environment.

After sanding, our highly trained crew will stain the hardwoods to any color you choose, which means you have the perfect opportunity to change the current stain color by either going darker or lighter. After staining we will apply the satin, semi-gloss or gloss finish to seal the floors and keep them looking beautiful for years. We will help you erase the memory of Hurricane Matthew while saving you money on the hardwood floor restoration as our pricing is very competitive. We have over 20 years experience in hardwood floor refinishing, wood floor restoration and installing hardwood floors and are considered one of the top companies in the industry.

Even if you sustained no damage from Hurricane Matthew, maybe it is time to install new hardwood floors in your home, or expand the usage of the current hardwood flooring into other areas of your home or business. Call Just Floored LLC of Jacksonville, Florida for a fast, no obligation quote.

Our workmanship is superior and are prices are the best in the area. This North Florida area hurt by the hurricane may include Florida cities like Jacksonville, St. Augustine, Ormond Beach, Hastings, Atlantic Beach, Northside, Middleburg, Fernandina Beach, Palm Coast, Gainesville, Orange Park, Jacksonville Beach, Palm Valley, Palatka, and more.
