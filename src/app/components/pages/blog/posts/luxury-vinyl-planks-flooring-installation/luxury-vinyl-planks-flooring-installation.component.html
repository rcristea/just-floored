Large Collection of Colors

Luxury Vinyl Plank Flooring comes in a large collection of colors and styles. Choose a grey tone in a wide plank, or a dark wood tone in a hand scraped style. The choices are endless and you can easily the right style and color to fit your home and furniture. Best of all, it is worry free, easy clean up, so it will last for years. Most people will assume they are real wood floors. But you will never have to have them refinished! And they should not be waxed.

Luxury Vinyl Plank Flooring will usually stay room temperature, so it won't feel cold on your bare feet. It is soft and spongy to walk on. Vinyl planks also absorb sound, so the floors will not echo and transmit noise like hardwoods can do. Luxury Vinyl Plank Flooring can be used anywhere in your home, from living room, kitchen, den, bathrooms, playrooms, bedrooms, basements, laundry rooms.
