Handscraped Hardwood Flooring

26 January 2016

Just Floored offers a wide variety of wood species, stain colors, wood plank widths and styles with the "Handscraped" look. Our selection has grown mostly because of the rising popularity of this look. We get calls everyday asking about installing hand-scraped floors and have seen first hand how great these floors look.

Hand-scraped flooring has the natural-looking chatter marks with the look of antique wear and tear. The distressing, scraping, worm holes and more, in different areas of each board create a unique charm that can be felt with the touch of the hand. Each flooring plank is unique in the distressed wood-grain look, showing natural imperfections, knots and mineral streaks. Hand scraped floors come in pre-finished hardwoods, engineered wood flooring and laminate flooring. Plank widths vary. Stain colors are available in a huge range. Species of wood including hickory, oak, cherry, walnut, maple, bamboo and laminate flooring.

The decorating effect on a room using handscraped flooring is remarkable. It adds a rich texture to the room without being busy and taking over the room. It goes with almost any decorating style, just choose the right wood species and stain color to give your home an individual look that will truly increase the value of your home. Just Floored, LLC. can install the hand-scraped floors in your home or business right away. Call us today for a free estimate. You will be surprised how affordable our services are. We have over 20 years experience in the Greater Atlanta Area and will provide you the best in installation services offered in the area. Our work is fully warranted as well.

Distressed Hardwood Flooring

Distressing hardwood floors is a time honored tradition of handcrafting that has seen the innovation of modern engineering and prefinishing wood flooring planks for a runaway popular look. Every wood plank of flooring is hand or machine sculpted giving it a rustic look of a rustic that is reminiscent of antique old-world charm.
