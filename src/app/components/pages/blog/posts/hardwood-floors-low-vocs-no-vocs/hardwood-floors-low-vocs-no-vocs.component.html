Hardwood Floors Low VOC's, No VOC's

10 July 2015

Just Floored, LLC is proud to offer low VOC and No VOC's wood floor products that have lower or no formaldehyde for a better, healthy environment. For years, formaldehyde has been used in the engineered wood floors as well as subfloors to fuse layers of real wood to layers of plywood, birch or pine in wood floors as well as wood floor finishes. VOC stands for Volatile Organic Compound which at room temperature emits an off gas into the air which is actually harmful to people's health. VOC's are responsible for damage to eyes, lungs, ears, nose and throat and can cause irritation, nausea, and in the long run, can cause nose or throat cancer. With all the pollutants already in the environment, the last thing a homeowner needs to do is bring a pollutant into their home in the form of an off gassing glue or wood floor finish, when low VOC or No VOC's products are available.

Just Floored offers "green" products that provide Low VOC or No VOC's wood flooring that has been made specifically to reduce these off gassing chemicals in the solid wood, engineered wood, and subfloors creation including the adhesives and polyurethane sealers. Many wood floor installers look for the cheapest products, some from China, without worrying about the long term effects these flooring products can create to the family living in the home. Just Floored can help you choose engineered wood flooring, subfloors and other flooring products that are have low VOC's or No VOC's water based finishes to no VOC's Rubio Monocoat finishes to keep your home "green" and safe for your family. And these safer products are not necessarily more expensive than the higher VOC off gassing products. Just Floored offers phenol formaldehyde products which is not toxic instead of urea formaldehyde products, call us today to learn more.

The easiest thing for a homeowner to remember when considering installing new wood flooring or refinishing their current floors is Low VOC's or No VOC's water based finishes for cleaner air in your home. Off gassing, the emitting of harmful gasses, is found in glues and wood floor sealers and coatings. Products from China are especially suspect. To make sure you have a low emitting floor, ask us about products that have:

  • No Added Formaldehyde (NAF)
  • No Added Urea Formaldehyde (NAUF)
  • Ultra Low-Emitting Formaldehyde (ULEF)
